Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Whole-Frame Tiled Print (“the App”), developed by Minghao Wang, handles information when you use the macOS application.

Effective date: July 6, 2026
Last updated: July 6, 2026

Summary: The App processes your images locally on your Mac. We do not collect, store, sell, or transmit your personal data or image content to our servers. The App does not include analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App is designed as a local utility. We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Account or login information (the App has no user accounts)
  • Image content or metadata from your files
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Location data
  • Contacts, calendars, or other personal data from your Mac
  • Health, financial, or sensitive categories of data

For App Store privacy disclosures, this app is categorized as collecting Data Not Collected by the developer.

Information Processed Locally on Your Device

When you use the App, the following data is processed only on your Mac and remains under your control:

DataPurposeStored by us?
Images you openLayout, preview, export, and print tilingNo — processed in memory; not sent to us
Print and layout settingsYour current session preferencesNo — not persisted to our servers
Exported filesSaved where you chooseNo — written only to locations you select
Printer namesListed via macOS print services for printingNo — read from the system; not transmitted to us

File Access

The App runs in the macOS App Sandbox and accesses files only when you explicitly choose them (Open, drag-and-drop, Export, or Save). The App does not scan directories or access files in the background without your action.

Entitlement: com.apple.security.files.user-selected.read-write — read/write access limited to files and folders you select through system dialogs.

Printing & Network Use

The App can send print jobs to printers configured in macOS. This communication occurs between the App and your Mac’s print subsystem (CUPS), including network printers you have added in System Settings.

  • Print entitlement: com.apple.security.print
  • Network client entitlement: com.apple.security.network.client — used solely so the system can reach network printers you configure; not used to contact developer-operated servers.

We do not receive your print jobs or image data over the internet.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media SDKs. Distribution is through the Mac App Store, which is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy for store-related processing (e.g., purchase, download).

Children’s Privacy

The App is a general-purpose creative/utility tool and is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention & Security

Because we do not collect personal data, we maintain no user databases. Image data exists only in the App’s local memory while in use and in files you save. We recommend using standard Mac security practices (FileVault, screen lock) to protect your device.

Your Rights

Depending on your region (e.g., GDPR, PIPEDA, CCPA), you may have rights regarding personal data. Since the developer does not collect personal data through the App, there is typically nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our systems. For App Store purchase records, contact Apple.

International Users

The App performs processing locally on your device. No cross-border transfer of your image content to the developer occurs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect app or legal changes. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. Material changes may also be noted in App Store release notes.
